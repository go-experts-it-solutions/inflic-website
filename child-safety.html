Child Safety Standards

Effective Date: May 2026

At Infliq, we are committed to maintaining a safe and secure platform for all users. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and strictly prohibit any content, behavior, or activity that harms minors.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

Infliq strictly prohibits:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)
  • Exploitation or abuse of minors
  • Grooming or predatory behavior
  • Sharing sexualized content involving minors
  • Solicitation involving minors
  • Any illegal activity related to child exploitation

Any violation of these standards will result in immediate action, including permanent suspension of accounts and reporting to appropriate law enforcement authorities where required.

2. Safety Measures

To protect our users and maintain platform safety, Infliq implements:

  • User reporting and blocking features
  • Content moderation systems
  • Account monitoring for suspicious behavior
  • Immediate review of abuse reports
  • Removal of prohibited content
  • Enforcement actions against violators

We continuously improve our moderation and safety systems to detect and prevent harmful activity.

3. Reporting Violations

Users can report inappropriate content, abuse, or suspicious activity directly within the app or by contacting us.

If you encounter any content or behavior that violates these standards, please report it immediately.

4. Contact Information

For child safety concerns or abuse reports, contact:

Email: info@infliq.com

5. Cooperation with Authorities

Infliq cooperates with applicable law enforcement agencies and child protection organizations when legally required regarding cases involving child exploitation or abuse.

6. Age Restrictions

Infliq is not intended for users below the minimum legal age required under applicable laws.

Users who do not meet the minimum age requirements are prohibited from using the platform.

7. Enforcement Actions

Violations of these standards may result in:

  • Content removal
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent account termination
  • Reporting to authorities

8. Updates to This Policy

Infliq may update these Child Safety Standards periodically to improve user safety and comply with legal requirements.
