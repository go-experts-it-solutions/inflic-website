Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your Infliq account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

Delete Your Account

Warning: Deleting your account is permanent. This action will:

  • Permanently remove your profile, photos, and videos.
  • Delete your follower and following lists.
  • Remove all your comments and likes.
  • Delete all messages and chat history.

How to delete via the App:

  1. Open the Infliq app.
  2. Go to your Profile.
  3. Tap Settings (gear icon).
  4. Select Account.
  5. Tap Delete Account.
  6. Confirm your password and tap Permanently Delete.

Delete via Web:

If you cannot access the app, please enter your email below to receive a deletion link, or contact our support at support@infliq.com.
